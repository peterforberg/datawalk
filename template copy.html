Core Components

Shared Purpose

“A feature of aligned systems in which sectors share a mutual understanding and commitment to a vision and priority outcomes.”

Key Findings

Shared purpose motivates aligning efforts.
ACHs can facilitate aligning by building shared purpose across sectors and geographic boundaries.
Shared purpose is measured here by survey respondent’s understanding of and commitment to the ACH’s vision, but it could also relate to smaller objectives like project or program outcomes.
Agreeing on an ACH’s shared purpose can be particularly challenging when the ACH serves racially, ethnically, or geographically diverse communities.
Almost all survey respondents said they were committed to their ACH’s vision but fewer said they could describe the vision and even fewer said they could describe how their ACH’s vision would be achieved.
Shared purpose was most strongly influenced by perceived effectiveness, power dynamics, and financing. For every unit increase in these indicators, shared purpose also increased.
